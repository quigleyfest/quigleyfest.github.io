Travel

Bus

We will run a bus from Newcastle city centre, leaving at approximately 12.30pm stopping at Wooler, to the venue to arrive at 14:15pm. And another bus to take people back at the end of the night, 12.30am. Please let us know with RSVP if you need a space on the bus!

Car

The venue is 1hr 30mins from Newcastle. There will be car parking for drivers at hall and at cottages.

Taxis

Taxis will be available to book but it is a good idea to book these ahead of time as mobile signal will be limited in the valley. The cottages in the valley do have landlines (these are olden days phone that plug into walls and don't rely on mobile phone signal)

Flights

Nearest airport is Newcastle Airport, There is no public transport directly between the venue and the airport but it is well linked with the Metro and taxis to Newcastle city centre. The metro takes about 30 minutes - remember to buy a ticket, there are no barriers at the airport (usually) but there are always ones in city centre stops!

Will update with details on the bus and put people in touch of lift shares are possible!
